Spell Bee
achieved first place in spell bee

Spell Bee Competition, in general, means a spelling contest where students are asked to spell the words correctly, with a varying degree of a medium, i.e., easy to difficulty. As written in dictionaries, contestants must memorize the spellings of words and recite them accordingly.

Cooking without fire
achieved third place in cooking without fire

It was conducted with a view to create awareness about the nutritional value of food cooked without fire. The students prepared healthy dishes like ‘Sprouted Bhel, Orange Tang and Cheesy Biscuit Canoapy’ using easy and quick recipes. They served the dishes in an artistic and aesthetic way.
